Paradise Massage
・Rating: 4.5
・Price: <strong><u>Good value / Affordable</u></strong>
・Recommended: Those wanting an affordable, authentic beachside massage experience.
This spot offers a truly unique, no-frills massage experience right on the beach, where the soothing sound of the waves provides a natural relaxation soundtrack. It's praised for its friendly, accommodating staff and especially its <strong><u>great value for money</u></strong>. Many regulars consider it the best on the island, coming back year after year. While it's an authentic and somewhat basic setup – don't expect luxury amenities or complete privacy – the prime location and the quality of the massage itself win many over. For those who prioritize the beach ambiance and an affordable, effective massage over spa-like frills, this is a fantastic, highly recommended choice.
