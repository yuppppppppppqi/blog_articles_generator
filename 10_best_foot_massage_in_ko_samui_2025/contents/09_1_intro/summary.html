Coconut Paradise Spa

  • Rating: 4.5
  • Price: Average/Mid-range (500-550 THB for deep foot/leg)
  • Recommended: Those wanting effective deep tissue or foot massage in a clean spa (potential nearby noise).

Located conveniently in Fisherman's Village, Coconut Paradise Spa offers a clean and professional environment for a relaxing treat. Visitors often highlight the skilled and friendly therapists, like Et and Noi, who are particularly effective at delivering deep tissue and foot massages that really hit the spot and relieve tension.

While it provides a solid massage experience at average mid-range prices, some guests note a potential downside: noise from nearby clubs can sometimes filter in, slightly impacting the otherwise tranquil atmosphere. However, for those seeking an effective, professional massage, especially targeting legs and feet, from capable hands in a pleasant setting, it remains a popular choice.
