Siam Elephant Massage

  • Rating: 3.7 / 5 (168 reviews)
  • Price: 180 THB for 60-min foot massage
  • Recommended: Budget-conscious travelers looking for no-frills traditional Thai massage experience

Located in Fortune Town Mall, Siam Elephant Massage stands out for its remarkably affordable prices in the Siam area. The establishment offers a basic but functional setting, focusing on traditional Thai massage techniques rather than luxury amenities.

While the quality of service can vary significantly between therapists, some provide surprisingly good massages for the price point. The mall location makes it easily accessible, though the environment can get busy and noisy during peak hours.

For those prioritizing value for money over ambiance, this no-frills massage spot can be a decent option, especially during off-peak times.
