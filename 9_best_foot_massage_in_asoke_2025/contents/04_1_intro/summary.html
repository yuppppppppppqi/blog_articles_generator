Loft Thai Spa & Massage - Sukhumvit 38

  • Rating: 4.9
  • Price: Premium/Expensive, positioned as a splurge or treat.
  • Recommended: Recommended for: Guests seeking a luxurious, high-end spa experience with meticulous service, beautiful aesthetics, and a wide range of treatments, especially facials and specialized packages. Ideal for special occasions or self-indulgence.

From the moment you enter, the boutique spa ambiance with its beautiful Thai-influenced design and calming energy sets a luxurious tone. The service is consistently described as exceptional and personalized, with staff like Roxy at reception and therapists Apple and Cherry receiving specific praise.

They offer a range of high-quality treatments, including various massages, cupping, and facials (Aesop, HydraFacial) which many reviewers felt left their skin radiant and glowing. The experience is enhanced by luxurious details like welcome drinks, detailed explanations, post-treatment snacks/tea, and private rooms with showers.

While it's definitely a premium price point, most feel it's a worthwhile splurge, though a few found the facials underwhelming regarding extraction. (136 words).
