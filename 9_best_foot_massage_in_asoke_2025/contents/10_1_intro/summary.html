Dio spa & massage

  • Rating: 4.8
  • Price: Mid-range to Premium ("Abit more expensive").
  • Recommended: Recommended for: Guests seeking highly skilled, professional massages in a very clean and quiet environment. Particularly good for those looking for effective deep tissue work or specialized treatments like 4-hand massage.

This spa is recognized for its top-notch skill level and cleanliness, attracting a discerning clientele. Housed in a calm, colonial-style building tucked away quietly, it offers a professional and relaxing atmosphere.

Therapists like Doung, MIB, and Nam are highlighted for providing effective, well-managed massages (including 4-hand and Thai) that expertly target muscle knots without unnecessary pain. The attentive service includes pre- and post-massage drinks.

While described as a bit more expensive, many find the quality justifies the price. One note mentions potentially less attentive service for local customers compared to foreigners, but overall, it's highly recommended for skilled, professional massages.
