ThaiThai Massage Sukhumvit24

  • Rating: 4.8
  • Price: Affordable/Good Value, especially with happy hour discounts (around 300-420 baht for 60 min).
  • Recommended: Recommended for: Budget-conscious visitors looking for a convenient, clean massage spot near the BTS/mall, particularly for foot or Thai massages during happy hour. Less ideal for those seeking a completely quiet environment or guaranteed consistency.

This branch offers a clean, modern facility with a distinct, cute beach-like vibe contributing to a relaxing atmosphere. The staff are generally seen as professional, well-trained, and providing good service.

Specific masseuses like Tae, Mo, Rat, and Poy are highlighted for giving strong, effective massages that successfully target aches, particularly Deep Tissue and 4-in-1 options. Its location near Phrom Phong BTS and Emporium Mall is a major plus for convenience.

Happy hour discounts make it particularly affordable. However, some inconsistency in massage quality, potential noise due to open walls, and occasional feelings of being rushed are mentioned drawbacks.

Cash only. (129 words).
