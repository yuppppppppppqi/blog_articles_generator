This place is consistently praised for its spotlessly clean venue, nice decor, and charming, respectful staff. Therapists, including names like June, Noon, Fong, and Kati, receive high marks for being well-trained, professional, attentive, and possessing strong hands for effective oil, Thai, and foot massages.

The Siamese Aromatherapy Oil Massage with Hot Herbal Compress is a popular choice. Its location near the Marriott Marquis makes it convenient for hotel guests, and they offer promotions like BOGO deals and accept credit cards, adding to the appeal.

While slightly pricier than some, the value is considered good, especially with deals, though some noted minor inconsistencies in pressure. (128 words).
