Jane & June Massage

  • Rating: 4.8
  • Price: Mid-range (Good value for service level)
  • Recommended: Visitors looking for a full-service spa experience (massage, nails, waxing) with excellent customer care, cleanliness, and convenience (free transport). Ideal for those wanting tailored, professional massages in a beautiful setting.

Jane & June Massage is a highly recommended, family-run business known for its exceptional, friendly service and beautiful, spotlessly clean environment. They truly go the extra mile, offering free hotel pick-up and drop-off, plus welcome drinks, making the whole experience seamless and relaxing.

Guests rave about the comprehensive packages (massage, facial, nails) and individual services like waxing, performed by highly skilled, professional, and knowledgeable staff who tailor pressure perfectly. It's praised as one of the best massage experiences, offering amazing treatments at good prices.

From the warm welcome to the post-treatment tea, everything contributes to a feeling of being incredibly well taken care of.
