eszence Massage & Spa

Rating: 4.9 ⭐ from over 4,500 reviews – HUGE number of reviews, very popular!

This spacious Sukhumvit venue boasts a calm, professional atmosphere. It feels upscale without being stuffy. Therapists like K. Pook and Ja-O truly personalize each session—from Swedish to aroma massages. You feel like they're really focusing on *you*.

  • Price: ~500 THB for 60 minutes – Still great value for the quality.
  • Location: Sukhumvit – Convenient for many.
  • Extras: Cooling tea to start, hot tea & fruit + yogurt to finish – Love these thoughtful extras!

As a Bangkok resident of three years, I highly recommend eszence for both therapeutic relief (they fixed my shoulder once!) and pure relaxation. It's a treat.
