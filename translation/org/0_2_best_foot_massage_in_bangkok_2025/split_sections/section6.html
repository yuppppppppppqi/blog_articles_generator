Nikko Thai Massage @ Ratchathewi

Rating: 4.9 ⭐ from over 1,000 reviews – Solid reputation!

With clean, spotless rooms and a calm atmosphere, this place opens early at 9 am—perfect after a long flight (I know that feeling!). Therapists like Gigi and Joy excel at finding and easing stubborn knots, from foot massages to hot stone treatments. They're miracle workers sometimes!

Located steps from BTS Ratchathewi, they serve hot tea post-session (a lovely tradition) and accept card payments (super convenient).

  • Price: ~450 THB for 60 minutes – Very reasonable!
  • Location: Near BTS Ratchathewi – Easy access.
  • Specialties: Foot, full body, herbal compress – Good variety.

As a Bangkok resident of three years, I highly recommend Nikko for a reliable, high-quality Thai massage experience. It's a dependable choice.
