Yin Yang The Original Massage & Spa

As part of my "10 Best Foot Massage Shops in Bangkok (2025)" list, Yin Yang The Original Massage & Spa truly shines. After three years in Bangkok, I've learned that a great massage is about more than technique—it's the whole experience, you know?

Here's what makes Yin Yang special in my book:

  • Expert therapists like Jun, Noo, and Amm blend strong, precise pressure with gentle stretches to melt away knots. Seriously, they find knots I didn't know I had!
  • The serene, spotless studio welcomes you with iced or hot barley tea, plus sweet mango slices for a perfect finish. It's these little touches!
  • Flexible options—from classic Thai and deep-tissue to herbal pouch scrubs—so you can customize every visit. I love having choices.
  • Great value: Prices start around 975 THB for 90 minutes, ideal for both tourists and expats. Quality doesn't always mean expensive!

Whether you need serious tension relief or a soothing foot reflexology session, Yin Yang delivers consistency and comfort every time. It's become a regular spot for me.
