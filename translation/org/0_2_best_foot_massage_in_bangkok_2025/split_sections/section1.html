Better Day Healthy Massage @ Novotel Sukhumvit 20

As a Bangkok resident of three years, I've found Better Day Healthy Massage inside Novotel Sukhumvit 20 to be one of my absolute favourites. Seriously, this place is a gem!

This spa offers a clean, peaceful setting on the 9th floor, with friendly reception staff who even ask your pressure preferences before the session - a nice touch, right? Therapists like Mon, Tukta, and Nammon blend traditional Thai and deep-tissue techniques to hit every single tension spot. They really know their stuff.

  • Spotless facilities: Hotel-level cleanliness you can trust. Super important to me!
  • Personalized pressure: Staff gently checks in during your massage. No awkwardness.
  • Reasonable rates: A five-star experience without the steep price tag. Love a good deal!

Whether you need deep relief or simple relaxation, Better Day delivers a consistent five-star experience for tourists and expats alike. I always leave feeling brand new.
