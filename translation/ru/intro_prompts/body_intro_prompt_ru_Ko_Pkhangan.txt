[マッサージ店の特徴まとめ]
Sri Pai Massage
・Rating: 4.9
・Highlights: Exceptionally skilled therapists who excel at identifying and resolving problem areas and trigger points. Known for thoroughness, use of effective balms (available for purchase), and genuinely sweet, kind staff. Offers complimentary yummy tea in a humble yet functional setting. Consistently rated as one of the best massages in Thailand by experienced travelers.
・Price: Standard/Affordable (mentioned as "unbeatable")
・Recommended: Visitors seeking high-quality, personalized therapeutic massages at great value, appreciating a warm, authentic atmosphere over luxury.

Pure Relax Sri Thanu
・Rating: 4.8
・Highlights: Features therapists with strong hand power, effective at relieving deep fatigue. Praised for clean facilities, professional/friendly staff, and relaxing music. Offers a variety of massages including quality oil and balm options, skillfully finding tension spots. Provides both strong and gentle approaches.
・Price: Standard
・Recommended: Guests looking for reliable, professional massages (especially oil/balm) in a clean, well-designed environment. Booking in advance is often necessary, particularly later in the day.

REVIVE Massage
・Rating: 4.6
・Highlights: Staffed by highly professional, attentive, and precise therapists (Tum, Bella, Som Si, With specifically mentioned). Bella noted by a massage therapist for finding all tension areas and working deep on fascia. Offers specialized treatments like Facelift Massage. Cozy atmosphere with post-massage tea and fruit. Known for targeted therapeutic work on back/shoulders.
・Price: Standard (stable pricing noted)
・Recommended: Individuals needing focused therapeutic massage for specific pain points, massage connoisseurs appreciating precision, or those interested in unique treatments like Facelift Massage.

San Rak Health massage
・Rating: 5.0
・Highlights: Universally praised for its incredibly warm, friendly, and professional team creating a welcoming, safe atmosphere. Features "healer" level therapists (Mama Mouse, Pipon, Ratporn) who provide blissful, effective massages without causing pain. Exceptional Thai body and foot massages noted. Caters well to those with sensitive nervous systems. Beautiful energy in the shop.
・Price: Standard/Affordable (mentioned as "great")
・Recommended: Anyone desiring a deeply caring, relaxing, yet highly effective massage, especially recommended for outstanding foot massages or those sensitive to aggressive techniques.

Boonma Health Massage
・Rating: 4.8
・Highlights: Offers a welcoming, positive, and peaceful atmosphere. Masseuses (Sao mentioned) are noted for being strong, attentive, and professional, applying perfect pressure for relaxation. Excellent foot & shoulder massages. Praised for cleanliness, well-trained staff, and providing relaxing tea. Also offers spa services like body scrubs and waxing.
・Price: Standard/Affordable (mentioned as "not expensive")
・Recommended: Guests seeking a strong yet deeply relaxing massage in a calm, clean setting. Particularly good for foot/shoulder work and as a preferred alternative to Pure Relax for some due to stronger pressure.

Pure Relax
・Rating: 4.6
・Highlights: Long-established salon with a reputation built over many years. Features skilled, professional, and experienced practitioners (JJ mentioned) offering good oil and Thai massages. Known for spa packages and ability to address specific issues. Convenient location with parking. Popular spot, booking recommended especially in high season.
・Price: Standard/Loyal Prices
・Recommended: Visitors seeking a reputable, well-established salon with diverse services including spa packages. Good for repeat visitors or those booking planned treatments. (Note: Some recent reviews mention inconsistency/service issues).

Amatrra Massage
・Rating: 4.8
・Highlights: Home to highly skilled masseuses, including Pon, noted as a "true healer" expert at finding hidden spots and relieving pain/muscle knots. Offers excellent foot massages with perfect pressure in comfy seats. Provides strong, effective, yet relaxing Thai massages balancing strength and softness. Calm atmosphere with a lovely team, serving tea/water after.
・Price: Standard
・Recommended: Individuals with specific muscle knots or tension needing expert attention, or those seeking a top-tier foot massage in a relaxing environment. Popular with expats.

Pure Relax Thong Sala
・Rating: 4.7
・Highlights: Therapist "Day" is highly recommended, especially for 2-hour Thai massages. Staff are knowledgeable, professional, and attentive, knowing where extra work is needed. Features a clean environment with a pleasant smell and relaxing hot tea post-massage. Offers sports massages for those wanting firmer pressure. Located centrally in Thong Sala.
・Price: Standard (300 baht mentioned for Thai)
・Recommended: Centrally located visitors in Thong Sala seeking skilled Thai or sports massages, particularly longer sessions. Honest staff noted for returning a lost phone. (Note: Some recent reviews mention shared rooms impacting privacy).

Double B Health Massage
・Rating: 4.9
・Highlights: Delivers a perfect blend of relaxation and therapeutic precision. Masseuses (Owner View/Wiu, Mo mentioned) are highly skilled, kind, and friendly, providing strong but not painful pressure tailored to individual needs. Very effective at relieving significant pain (e.g., shoulder/thigh). Warm, welcoming, quiet atmosphere. Excellent Thai, oil, and foot massages reported.
・Price: Standard/Affordable
・Recommended: Guests looking for massages that are both deeply relaxing and therapeutically effective, especially those who prefer strong but comfortable pressure to address pain or tension.
