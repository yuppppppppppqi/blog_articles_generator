Grand Massage and Spa - Nana

  • Rating: 4.6 / 5 (205 reviews)
  • Price: Upper mid-range (600-2,500 THB)
  • Recommended: Perfect for those seeking a luxurious spa experience in the Nana area. Ideal for customers who appreciate upscale amenities, private treatment rooms, and late-night availability.

Situated in the vibrant Nana district, Grand Massage and Spa offers a premium massage experience in an elegant setting. The establishment distinguishes itself with luxurious amenities and thoughtful touches, from welcome drinks to post-massage tea service.

Their therapists deliver consistently high-quality treatments, particularly excelling in hot stone and aromatherapy massages. The spa's late operating hours and professional atmosphere make it a popular choice for both tourists and locals seeking quality relaxation.

While prices reflect the upscale environment, the comprehensive service and refined ambiance justify the investment.
